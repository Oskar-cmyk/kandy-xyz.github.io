Bolne roke, mrzlično streljanje

Bolne roke, mrzlično streljanje

Digital-analog collage, created for exhibition “Novomeška pomlad” cosmic anarchism. I was interested, how to interpret Anton Podbevšek poetry. I was most impressed by the verse “sick hands feverish shooting”. Quickly I got an idea how to show that scene. A poet wrote what war looks like, without having experienced it, like him I created a visual of how I imagine the war of the future.

Spoznati Avtonomno tovarno ROG

Spoznati Avtonomno tovarno ROG Understand autonomous factory Rog,

Živko Skvotec and Modri kot, were two associations that squatted their premises in the Autonomous factory Rog. Collaboration between academy of fine art and Faculty of Social Science we tried to understand trough research, how this two associations connected, and differentiated. Visualisation of blue and red waveforms, intersecting in some 4-dimension, where together they build a space with their common points where they intersect. There is a great tension in these points, with which both complement each other’s disagreements.
