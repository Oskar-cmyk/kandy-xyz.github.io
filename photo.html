Avalanche_beta_1.3

Avalanche_beta_1.3
2022

The title Avalanche_beta_1.3 was given to the work because of the way it originated. It is created just like programs, endlessly flawed, but at the same time creates a sense of a holistic image. Vague answers and open interpretation trigger an uneasy feeling of not knowing. The avalanche is not supported by the audio recording of the actual avalanche, but by the distorted tones of an unknown instrument. Together with the video, they form something unknown in everyday life.

Bolne roke, mrzlično streljanje

Bolne roke, mrzlično streljanje
2021

Digital-analog collage, created for exhibition “Novomeška pomlad” cosmic anarchism. I was interested, how to interpret Anton Podbevšek poetry. I was most impressed by the verse “sick hands feverish shooting”. Quickly I got an idea how to show that scene. A poet wrote what war looks like, without having experienced it, like him I created a visual of how I imagine the war of the future.

frotage-Loop

frotage-Loop
2023

A zine with scans and photos using the frottage technique, reflecting life's journey—thickening, changing, and then thinning out.
Frottage images depict everyday objects, especially wood textures from my renovated apartment. I aimed to infuse meaning into these IKEA items that felt empty to me.

Spoznati Avtonomno tovarno ROG

Spoznati Avtonomno tovarno ROG
2021

Živko Skvotec and Modri kot, were two associations that squatted their premises in the Autonomous factory Rog. Collaboration between academy of fine art and Faculty of Social Science we tried to understand trough research, how this two associations connected, and differentiated.

Visualisation of blue and red waveforms, intersecting in some 4-dimension, where together they build a space with their common points where they intersect. There is a great tension in these points, with which both complement each other’s disagreements.

Triptih
2020

Digital photography, collage, ALUO Photoshop collage, of 4 photos, background and triptych. On photos is refinement of land, removing soil from rock. Rocks are left in their first position after refinement, meanwhile unknown person is taking away refined soil to place not visible to the viewer.
